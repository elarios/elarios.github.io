My research interest is in the field of Software Engineering and Big Data, in particular, Software Analytics. The complex management of near real-time events generated from hundreds of data sources involved in the software development life cycle demands for further research in the domains of mining software repositories and software analytics. My work aims to tackle current issues in (1) data integration from both development time and run-time data sources, (2) lack of a unified abstraction layer for processing and querying large volume of historical and near real-time software data and (3) difficulty in providing the right data (feedback) to all stakeholders at a proper time.
